Meet ThingyTag

ABOUT US

ThingyTag is a product of and for those who are passionate about their prized possessions

and those who consider them as an integral part of their own self and those who takes care of them as one of their body part.

We Value your Valuables. It is why our thoughtful founders have carefully crafted Your ThingyTag to serve and solve day-to-day circumstantial misplacements, and human forgetfulness, to help you retrieve your possession at your fingertips.

How ThingyTag works?

Buy ThingyTag and attach it to your valuables.

ThingyTag provides you with unique sets of QR code stickers & dangles for attaching it to any of your valuable possessions like phone, luggage, sports gear, etc.

Finders can scan your ThingyTag with their phone camera.

When any Individual who finds your unattended or misplaced object, he can scan the QR, with their phone camera.

Get instant alert from the finder through message or call.

You will receive the necessary information alert through Call & Messages immediately after you are notified by the finder of your ThingyTag'ed valuable.

Contact the finder and collect your valuables.

You can contact the person who has your valuable through ThingyTag and the location may also be available to you.
